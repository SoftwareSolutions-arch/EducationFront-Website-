The Central Board of Secondary Education has made changes in the exam pattern for English subject for Class 12 students. As per an official notification shared by CBSE in this regard major changes have been introduced in the ‘Reading Section’ of the English Core subject for the CBSE board exams that are scheduled to be conducted in February to March, 2019.

Regarding the same CBSE has issued an official notification stating: “Consequently upon the feedback received from the various stakeholders and subsequent deliberation in the meetings of Committee of courses in the Board and the subject experts, the following changes have been made in Section A- Reading of the Question Paper for English Core, Class XII for the session 2018- 2019”.

The changes made in English Core for Class 12 Board Exams 2019 are as mentioned below:

CBSE Class 12 Board Exam 2019: Changes in English Pattern

Section A- Reading

Old Pattern

New Pattern for 2018-2019

The number of comprehension passages has been reduced. Now only two passages would be included here.
  • Two passages of 1100- 1200 words were asked
  • On passage of 400-500 words was there
  • Two passages of 800-900 words each will be asked

 

Typology of questions in passages
  • 6 MCQs of one mark each
  • 16 very short answer type questions including one question on vocabulary which is of one mark weightage
  • One short answer type question of 3 marks
  • One long answer type question of 5 marks

Passage 1:

  • 5 MCQs of one mark each
  • 9 very short answer type questions including three questions on vocabulary of one mark each
  • 3 short answer type questions of two marks each

 

Passage 2:

  • 2 long answer type questions of 5 marks each
