Placements : My batch of Aerospace Engineers will be passing out in April, 2022. Last time I checked 85% of students sitting in placements GOT placed. In core jobs people work at positions as high as scientists in ISRO and many more. The highest salary goes up to 50 Lacs per year in core jobs and even more for non core jobs. The lowest salary packages are roughly around 6 Lacs per year, and the median salary goes around 10 Lacs. The top recruiting companies of the course are Airbus, Jaguar, Mathworks, General Electrics, Samsung etc. About 88% of students applied for an internship GOT selected in to companies like GE, Samsung, Boeing etc.

Infrastructure : The facilities available at my branch are of world class level. The lecture halls (classrooms), library, Labs, Student's Lounge, Canteens are all well illuminated, air-conditioned, have high-speed and secure Wi-Fi to cater the needs of students. The infrastructure here are constructed keeping in view of the future needs. The infrastructure and facilities available are extremely helpful to maintain an atmosphere of knowledge. We at IITK have our own airstrip and a hanger with 5 ready to takeoff planes to serve the research and learning objectives of students. My hostel has excellent facilities of rooms, drinking water, elevators to reach higher floors, all time electricity. The mess food is well above average. You will not your food quality is compromised in any way. We have our own health center to maintain health inside the campus. The sports facilities here are awesome. No one would even think that IITs may have such good infrastructure in sports, we have almost all olympic outdoor and indoor games with coaches for each games separately.

Faculty : The teachers are more then helpful in teaching and assisting in studies. All of them are doctorate degree holders from many Indian and foreign institutes and universities. They are well qualified and are knowledgeable to take the concerned courses. The teaching quality is above expectations. The lectures and weekly tutorials gives a person the perfect way to approach different problems and solve them. The course curriculum is very much relevant to the current and future need of the nation and society and of course the need of today's industries. That is why IITs have such good reputation in teaching and research.

Other : I choose Aerospace Engineering out of my interest in aircrafts and their flying. There is no way that I could find a lacuna in the environment and people in here. I would give our institute a 10/10 for the above mentioned fields like events, fests, crowd, connectivity, scholarships and extracurricular activities.

Placements : As it's an IIT, the placements here are quite good. Stats of every year show that almost 80% of the students get placed. This year, the highest salary package offered in off-campus placements was 1.2 crores per annum, and it was 70 lacs per annum in on-campus placements. The average salary package offered was around 12 lacs per annum. Companies that visited the campus were Microsoft, Tower, Citi Bank, Samsung, etc. The whole placement process was organised by a team of students. There are no exact stats about internships, but according to my knowledge, the highest stipend offered is about 1.50 lac per month. We have a very good placement cell here which efficiently manages all the placements. The internships are very useful for jobs indeed.

Infrastructure : IIT Kanpur has surely the best infrastructure and facilities in the country. Name a facility, and you have it here. Here, we have Wi-Fi enabled hostels, highly advanced lab for every field, and sports ground for every sport. The college has its own swimming pool, a health centre and an airstrip. Food served here is way better than what you can expect from any hostel, and the prices are also very reasonable. Every hostel has its own canteen. You won't find any issues with the infrastructure.

Faculty : Professors here are highly qualified whether it be theoretical or practical. Most of them have 10 -20 years of teaching experience. Teaching here is very well-planned, they plan what and how much exactly they have to teach every day. Teaching here is done with every teaching method you can think of like digital boards, green boards, some live practical sessions in class, video lectures, etc. The exact faculty to student ratio is not known, but you won't have to wait to clear your doubts as there are tutors for that here. We do go on industrial trips for subjects which require industrial visits. The course structure here is very flexible for most of the departments. You can choose and learn whichever subject you want to. Although, there are some courses that are mandatory.

Other : Life at IIT Kanpur is full of fun; it's just that you have to manage your academics along with it. The fests conducted here are very popular like Techkriti which is Aisa's largest technical fest, Udgosh which is India's largest sports fest, Antragni and many more. There are many clubs and hobby groups you can think of, and you can even start a club if you are interested. IIT Kanpur gives you the opportunity to explore every field whether it be academics or sports or music or film. Every hobby and every talent can be nurtured here. We have various scholarship programmes too. The college surroundings are full of greenery, and the campus area is about 1,100 acres.

Other : Many fests are organised, i.e. Antaragni is the annual cultural fest, Techkriti is an annual technological fest, Udghosh is the annual sports fest.
